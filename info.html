About Alpcoin (ALP)

Alpcoin is a humanitarian-focused token built on the TON blockchain. It aims to empower people through financial inclusion, charity initiatives, and community-driven projects.

Token Utility

  • Support charitable causes and transparent donations
  • Access to Alpcoin ecosystem and future dApps
  • Rewards for community participation and referrals

Vision

“Kelajakni birga quramiz” — Together, we build a future where blockchain empowers people, not just profit.
